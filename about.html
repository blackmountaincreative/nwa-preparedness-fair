ABOUT NWA PREPAREDNESS
Since 2007, the NWA Emergency Preparedness Fair has helped residents of Northwest Arkansas obtain the information they need to prepare their families, neighborhoods, and community for emergencies.
Emergencies can be minor or catastrophic. Personal or widespread. The NWA Emergency Preparedness Fair provides attendees with the tools and knowledge they need to confidently respond to any emergency.
The NWA Emergency Preparedness Fair is a great place for the whole family to come and learn about what to do in an emergency in a fun and educational way. Classes by local meteorologists, safety demonstrations, and an area for kids including games, crafts, and prizes.
