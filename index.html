5 REASONS TO BE THERE

EXTREME WEATHER

NWA is home to 5 types of extreme weather. Local Weather Experts Dan Skoff and Darby Bybee teach how to keep your family safe from all of them.

POISON CONTROL

In 2015, 2 million poison related emergencies were reported in the U.S. Come listen to Mr. Charlie Strut talk about the top situations to watch out for and how to respond in case your family encounters an exposure.

CAREER CHANGES

38% of jobs in the U.S. are considered HIGH RISK of being replaced by automation. Learn how to stay relevant in the toughtest job markets.

SELF DEFENSE

NWA is relatively safe, but there are still over 300 violent crimes committed every year in NWA. Learn how to identify and escape from predators who might hurt you or your family.

GIVE BLOOD (OPENS FRIDAY)

Every 2 seconds someone in the U.S. needs blood to save their life. To avoid wait time, reserve your time slot in advance. Use sponsor code: LDSBENTONVILLE. Blood drive opens Friday Sept. 22 at 12pm and runs through Saturday 23 at 3pm.
